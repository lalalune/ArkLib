# DP24

## At A Glance

`DP24` is the current binary-tower multilinear-proof reference cited in ArkLib's Binius
development.
It is the main paper page to consult when reviewing or extending the `ProofSystem/Binius` subtree.

DP24's **ring-switching** reduction has been factored out of the Binius subtree into a generic,
instantiable compiler under `ProofSystem/RingSwitching/`, with Binius as one instance. See the
concept page [`../concepts/ring-switching.md`](../concepts/ring-switching.md) and the blueprint
section *Ring Switching* (`blueprint/src/proof_systems/ring_switching.tex`).

## What ArkLib Uses From This Paper

- High-level problem framing and protocol lineage for Binius-related developments.
- Reference context for the binary-tower multilinear proof system components in ArkLib.
- The **ring-switching reduction** (§2.5 eq̃/trace identity; §3.1–3.2 soundness errors): packing a
  small-field multilinear into a large-field one and reducing the evaluation claim. The §2.5 identity
  is formalized as the `decomposeRows_spec`/`decomposeColumns_spec` laws of `RingSwitchingProfile`,
  proven for the Binius (tensor-algebra) instance.
