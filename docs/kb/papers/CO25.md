# CO25

## At A Glance

`CO25` is the Chiesa--Orru duplex-sponge Fiat-Shamir paper. ArkLib uses it as the source for the
duplex-sponge challenge oracle, codec and salt interfaces, Construction 4.3, and the Section 5
security framework around Lemma 5.1.

## What ArkLib Uses From This Paper

- The duplex-sponge Fiat-Shamir construction over a start oracle and ideal permutation.
- Protocol codecs mapping prover messages into the sponge alphabet and sponge outputs into
  verifier challenges.
- The salted DSFS wrapper and the salt-codec bridge between on-sponge and FS-standard views.
- The trace/prover transformation framework and key-lemma residual surface for the Section 5
  hybrid proof.
