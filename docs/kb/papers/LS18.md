# LS18

## At A Glance

`LS18` is the Lyubashevsky–Seiler EUROCRYPT paper showing that, for a prime `q ≡ 5 (mod 8)` and
power-of-two degree, nonzero ring elements with small norm are invertible in
`Z_q[X]/(X^d + 1)`. This is what makes the relaxation factors in the weak-binding extractor
genuinely invertible.

## What ArkLib Uses From This Paper

- Corollary 1.2: short elements (centered Euclidean norm `< √q`) are units in `Rq Φ`,
  recorded as `isUnit_of_l1Norm_le`.
