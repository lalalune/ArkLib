# NS24

## At A Glance

`NS24` is the Nguyen–Seiler CRYPTO paper introducing Greyhound, a lattice polynomial commitment
built from an inner/outer Ajtai commitment composition. ArkLib formalizes that inner-outer
commitment and its weak-binding reduction to Module-SIS.

## What ArkLib Uses From This Paper

- The inner/outer commitment composition (inner Ajtai commit under `A`, gadget-decompose,
  outer commit under `B`).
- The weak-binding extractor structure (per-block challenges, scaled-message witness).

## Open Formalization Gaps

- The weak-binding bound relies on the deferred norm/invertibility inputs (`Mic07`, `LS18`).
- Only the commitment layer is formalized; the split-and-fold evaluation protocol is not.
